Announcing New Bounty System [ended on June 19, 2017]

Published by CRAVE Community at May 15, 2017
Categories
  • News
Tags

We are pleased to announce the following bounty system.


400 CRAVE for a working MAC wallet.

350 CRAVE for a working Android wallet.

40 CRAVE for translations (new thread on BitcoinTalk) to other languages.

2 CRAVE for every new member on the Facebook group. - LIMITED OFFER.

1 CRAVE for positive mentioning about Crave at a different BitcoinTalk thread (link must be provided). Max 3x per member. - LIMITED OFFER

1 CRAVE for Tweeting about CRAVE, retweeting, and mentioning our Twitter account @CraveProject.

MASTERNODE - If you buy at least 480 Crave on Cryptopia and send them to your local wallet to run a MN, the rest (20+1 CRAVE) will be paid to that wallet!

Feel free to suggest others!


Claims will no longer be attended to at the moment. This bounty system has ended on June 19, 2017.
